📇 AI-Powered Business Card Scanner & Lead Manager

A Streamlit-based smart lead capture system that scans business cards, extracts contact details using OCR, detects duplicates, assigns leads to teams, and triggers automated notifications.

🚀 Project Overview

This project is built as part of the mini project requirement. The system replaces manual business card entry with an automated AI-assisted workflow.

It allows teams to:

Scan business cards at on-campus events or meetups
Automatically extract Name, Email, Phone, Company
Detect duplicates (based on email)
Assign the lead to Placement Cell or Sales Team
Notify the owner through email
Track 48-hour follow-ups
Analyze leads using simple dashboards
Calculate a basic lead score
